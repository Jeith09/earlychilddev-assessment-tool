About This Assessment Tool

The Early Childhood Developmental Assessment Tool is designed to help educators and parents identify potential developmental concerns in children aged 3-6 years.

Purpose

  • Provide early screening for common developmental concerns
  • Guide conversations between teachers and parents
  • Identify children who may benefit from additional support
  • Track developmental progress over time

What This Tool Assesses

  • Attention & Focus: ADHD indicators
  • Social Communication: ASD indicators
  • Learning Development: Learning difficulty indicators
  • Emotional Regulation: Anxiety indicators
  • Behavioral Patterns: ODD indicators

Important Note

This is a screening tool only and does not provide medical diagnosis. Results should be discussed with qualified healthcare professionals for proper evaluation and diagnosis.

How to Use

  1. Complete all student information fields
  2. Answer each assessment question based on observations
  3. Review the generated report
  4. Share results with parents and relevant professionals
  5. Use recommendations to support the child's development
